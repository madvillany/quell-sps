Saul Denenberg recently joined Washington Square Realty Capital as a Senior Director and will be working in both business development and capital advisory services.

Saul Denenberg joined Washington Square Realty Capital as a Senior Director and will be working in both business development and capital advisory services. Prior to joining Washington Square, Saul spent over 20 years pursuing international commerce developing businesses both locally and at the global level.

He founded numerous start-up companies that were sold to both private investors and Fortune 50 companies. His success stems from surrounding himself with diverse, energetic and creative people. Saul believes that life and business are about individuals, relationships and constantly learning from others to keep ideas and the organizations that pursue them dynamic, innovative and inspiring. Saul is translating his past experience and acumen into serving real estate investors, developers and owners to secure capital in creative structures that meet the needs of investors while providing the flexibility and creativity needed to pursue modern real estate investments and developments.

Mr. Denenberg graduated in 1992 from the University of Arizona and is a 1987 graduate from Lower Merion High School. He currently resides in Montgomery County, Pennsylvania with his wife (Abbie) and daughter (Dylan).
