Chris Datz has been an active real estate professional and principal since 1997. Chris began his career as an Asset Manager at Philadelphia based Brandywine Construction & Management, Inc (“BCMI”). While at BCMI, Chris was actively involved in the leasing and management function for multiple trophy class assets including The Philadelphia Stock Exchange Building, The National Press Building and 1845 Walnut Street in Philadelphia. After BCMI, Chris joined the Flynn Company, a full service Philadelphia real estate service platform where he ran a 1 million square foot industrial portfolio in Central Pennsylvania for a pension fund client of the firm.

In 2001, Chris formed Pintzuk Brown Realty Group, where the partnership acquired and developed approximately 20 properties comprised of approximately 1 million square feet, valued at approximately $200MM. In his career Chris has been actively involved in hundreds of real estate transactions, comprised of multiple asset classes of property, with aggregate value approaching $1 Billion.

Since 2012, Chris has been utilizing his career experience to actively secure debt and equity on behalf of private and institutional clients.
