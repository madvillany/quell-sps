Garrett Miller

Managing Principal & Founder
Garrett Miller has worked in all facets of real estate during his 15 year career including: finance, development, asset management, leasing, property management and construction.

Garrett Miller has worked in all facets of real estate during his 15 year career including: finance, development, asset management, leasing, property management and construction. His background as a principal investor and developer enables him to fully comprehend all aspects of a transaction to identify the best solution. He has secured more than $300MM of debt and equity on real estate transactions as a principal investor and has helped to arrange over $400MM of debt and equity in the past 4 years as a capital advisor and financing broker.

A representative sample of his transactions include (P – Principal):

  • Curtis Center: Philadelphia, PA, 885,000 sf Office building, $110MM of debt and equity
  • Public Ledger Building: Philadelphia, PA, 435,000 sf Office Building, $50MM of debt and equity
  • Sanatoga Village Shoppes: Pottsville, PA, 48,000 sf Shopping Center, $8.5MM of debt (P)
  • Warminster Plaza: Warminster, PA, 112,000 sf Shopping Center, $19MM of debt
  • Lansdale Plaza: Lansdale, PA, 138,000 sf Shopping Center, $32MM of debt
  • Diamond Green, Philadelphia, PA, 300 Bed Student Housing, $22.5M Bank Loan
  • 1499 Christopher Columbus Blvd: Philadelphia, PA, 15 acre Land Parcel, $15MM Bridge Loan
  • The Rialto Building, Philadelphia, PA, 38,000 sf Office Building, $7.4MM CMBS Loan
  • 1914 Rittenhouse: Philadelphia, PA, 15,000 sf townhome, $9.5MM Construction Loan
  • Kirkbride Center: Philadelphia, PA, 300,000 sf Medical Campus, $13.0MM Bridge Loan
  • iStorage: National, Self-Storage Portfolio, $8.0MM Preferred Equity Investment
  • Lake Geneva Estate: Lake Geneva, WI, Golf Course & Land, $5.0MM Bridge Loan

Over the past 15 years Mr. Miller has worked with various national and local firms including: Mount Lucas Management (1999 – 2001) as an analyst; CB Richard Ellis (2002 – 2006) as a leasing associate primarily working as a landlord representative leasing office space throughout Midtown Manhattan and Harbor Plaza in Jersey City, NJ; Walnut Street Capital (2006 – 2009) as the head of the development and asset management teams; Hill International Real Estate Partners (2009 – 2011) as the President of the development group that proposed the 1.8MM sf American Commerce Center.

Mr. Miller is a former member of the US Olympic Team having competed in the 2000 Sydney Olympic Games where he placed 5th as a member of the Men’s Open 8+. He is a three-time World Champion having won Gold as a member of the Men’s Open 8+ that won World Championships in 1997 (Lac d'Aiguebelette, France), 1998 (Cologne, Germany) and 1999 (St. Catherines, Canada) and a three-time CRASH-B Sprints World Indoor Rowing Collegiate Champion in 1997, 1998 and 1999 and Silver Medalist in the Open Men’s International Final in 1999. He was a Captain of the University of Pennsylvania Rowing Team (1998 – 1999) and led them to the Ivy League and Eastern Sprints Championship in 1998. He was recently inducted into the United States Rowing Hall of fame as a member of the 2014 class.

He graduated in 1999 with a Bachelors of Science in Economics from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. He currently resides in Center City Philadelphia with his wife (Jennifer) and their daughters (Chloe & Sydney).
