Anthony Franchi

Senior Director
Anthony Franchi is Senior Director in charge of National Originations for Washington Square and will oversee a network of regional origination teams.

Anthony draws from his extensive skill set and experience in construction and finance to support Washington Square's advisory platform and build the firm's network of industry relationships.

He began his professional life in the construction industry with MMC, where he supported commercial site work and transportation infrastructure projects by sourcing essential materials and managing a complex logistics network moving over 1MM tons of aggregate and construction waste. From there he joined the mergers and acquisitions field where he served private equity firms and other strategic buyers by identifying valuable acquisition targets and originating M&A transactions totaling more $200MM.

Anthony currently resides in Philadelphia, PA.
